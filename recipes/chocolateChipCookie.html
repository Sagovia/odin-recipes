Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

This is a simple recipe for some classic chocolate chip cookies. They can be made thinner if you prefer a crunchier cookie, but can also be made in thicker chunks if you prefer a chewier, softer center. Feel free to experiment and add nuts, or toppings to add to the flavor.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven (to around 350°F or 180°C)
  2. In a large bowl, cream together softened butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated, and then add vanilla extract
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking soda, and salt
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix
  6. Gently fold in the chocolate chips (and nuts, if using)
  7. Use a cookie scoop or tablespoon to drop rounded portions of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for the specified time or until the edges are golden brown
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely
  10. Once cooled, enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite beverage!
