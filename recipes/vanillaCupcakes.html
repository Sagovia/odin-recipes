Vanilla Cupcake

Description

Vanilla cupcakes are an easy go-to desert if you're not too experienced in baking but want to make something that still tastes decent.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven (usually around 350°F or 180°C). Line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. Gather and measure all the ingredients according to the recipe.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. This can be done with an electric mixer or by hand.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  5. Mix in the vanilla extract to enhance the flavor of the cupcakes.
  6. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing just until combined. Alternate with adding the milk, starting and ending with the dry ingredients. Be careful not to overmix.
  8. Spoon or scoop the batter into the prepared cupcake liners, filling each about two-thirds full.
  9. Place the cupcake tin in the preheated oven and bake for the time specified in the recipe, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a cupcake comes out clean.
  10. Allow the cupcakes to cool in the tin for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Once the cupcakes are completely cool, you can frost them with your favorite icing or enjoy them plain.
  12. Serve your vanilla cupcakes and enjoy them as a delightful treat!
